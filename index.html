About

A three-day meeting of the research network for the interdisciplinary study of predictive processing in memory and perception (PPiMP), funded by the DFG. The programme brings together talks, keynotes, and a poster session, opening with a welcome reception on Tuesday evening and closing on Thursday at midday.
